About Me

Who am I?

I am a Software Engineer, CS Student, Surfer, design geek, and Lover of Nature.
As a lead programmer for the Smartfin team at UCSD's Scripps Institute of Oceanography, I have experience building web API's, User Interfaces, and many other skills on the web stack. I like to think out of the box putting my own touch and style in everything that I do and the problems that I solve.
My love of surfing has manifested itself into a strong interest in environmental protection. I believe that through science and engineering we can build a sustainable future for the generations to come and protect the planet that has given us so much.
